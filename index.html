OFFERING NO CONTACT DELIVERY

Step 1

ORDER ONLINE, USE OUR APP OR CALL.
 When placing your order online or through our app, leave us a comment in the delivery instructions that says “No Contact Delivery” and specify your desired location for our driver to place your order. When placing your order over the phone, let a team member know you’d like No Contact Delivery.

Step 2

Our QUALITY SEAL is applied to the lid of the box so you know it hasn’t been opened.

Step 3

Your delivery driver will place your order in your desired location.

Step 4

Your delivery driver will wait 6 FEET AWAY to ensure you received your meal.
